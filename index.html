about us

Discover our premier resort lodge in Mutoko, a fast-growing city that offers a vibrant atmosphere and promising opportunities. Nestled amidst serene surroundings, our lodge is an ideal destination for both relaxation and productive corporate events. With state-of-the-art conference facilities, our spacious meeting rooms provide a conducive environment for successful conferences and seminars. Our dedicated event staff will ensure seamless planning and execution, allowing you to focus on your objectives.

Indulge in exquisite cuisine at our on-site dining options and enjoy recreational activities in our wondeful landscape. With Mutoko's thriving business hubs, shopping centers, and cultural attractions within easy reach, our lodge provides convenience and accessibility for both business and leisure travelers. Immerse yourself in the essence of warm hospitality and make your stay exceptional at our resort lodge in Mutoko.

frequently asked questions

what are your payment methods?

At our resort, we accept a variety of convenient payment methods to ensure a seamless experience for our guests. You can choose to pay with credit cards, debit cards, mobile money services, bank transfers, or cash (in local currency or major foreign currencies, upon prior arrangement).

What amenities does the resort offer?

Our resort offers a wide range of amenities including swimming pools, spa, restaurants, bars, concierge services, and more.

Are there any dining options available at the resort?

Yes, we have multiple dining options at our resort, including a fine dining restaurant and a poolside bar serving snacks and beverages. Please note that breakfast is complimentary that is it is inclusive in the booking fee.

Is Wi-Fi available throughout the resort?

Yes, we offer complimentary Wi-Fi access throughout the resort, allowing you to stay connected during your stay.

Can I make reservations online?

In a matter of time, yes, our IT crew is working tirelessly to come up with an online system where you can easily make reservations online through our website.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Our resort is open 24hrs and you can check in at any given time subject to availability. it should however be brought to your attention that check out time is 10am

Is parking available at the resort? Is there a fee?

Yes, we provide complimentary on-site parking for our guests during their stay. There are no additional fees for parking.

What is the cancellation policy for reservations?

Our cancellation policy may vary depending on the type of reservation and time of year. Please refer to our website or contact our reservations department for detailed information regarding our cancellation policy.
